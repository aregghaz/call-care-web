What We Offer

Ambulatory Service

Ambulatory Service is mobility at your front door. People who have
difficulties walking are able to be assisted by GMT staff. From the
front door to our vehicle a trained GMT staff member will be with our
clients every step of the way. GMT vehicles are comfortable, clean,
odor free and undergo regular service checks to ensure the highest
level of comfort and safety as our clients are transported to their
destination. Our vision is to provide quality access to quality care.

What We Offer

Wheelchair Services

General Medical provides exceptional wheelchair transportation
to fit the needs of our clientele. General Medical vehicles
are equipped with electric wheelchair ramps and lifts that
allows easy entry and exit from our vehicles. GMT can also
provide its wheelchair bound clients with an EMT, if
necessary, to ensure the highest level of care.
